Recipe

Description

The most fluffy and delicious pancakes you will ever eat! Even a Nobel Prize winner would not say no to this.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Melt butter the butter and add sugar while whisking.
  2. Crack the eggs an separate the whites from yolk. Add the yolk to the mix and whisk the whites until it becomes fluffy.
  3. Add Honey to the mix and after whisking add the fluffy whites.
  4. Now add some flour and have rather too little than too much.
  5. Now fry the pancakes and serve with berries
